Marketing Automation Platform

100% built in Python (Django)

I developed a tailor made marketing automation platform for one of ENGIE's startups (Please).

The platform included the following features: user tracking solution, user data enrichment, data lake, automated email, SMS and push notification workflows, A/B testing, dynamic landing pages (linked to SEA campaigns) and a real-time Data Analytics platform (built with the Amplitude Analytics API).

I tracked 15+ million events in real-time, enriched the profile of 250k+ users, sent tens of million push notifications, SMS and emails (fully automated).
